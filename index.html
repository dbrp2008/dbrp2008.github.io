Drag a part onto the grid. Pipes lock to grid points.

○ amber rings mark open ends; ● green dots mark connected ends. To join parts, drag one part's end onto the other's amber ring (the same grid point). An elbow joins at its corner dot — bring the pipe's end onto it.

A Branch taps a lock point off the pipe it sits on, with its own amber ring another pipe can connect to. Drop it anywhere, then drag it onto a pipe run — it stands itself perpendicular to the run.

Connected parts are locked together and drag as one assembly. Right-click a joint to get a Separate option — the two sides come apart, each keeping its own parts locked together. Rotate with the handle (45° steps) or press R. Double-click deletes.

◯ Select in the toolbar lets you circle several parts — then change the size, orientation or colour of everything inside at once.
