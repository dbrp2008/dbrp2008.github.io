ℹ️ How billing costs are calculated

Monthly - One charge per month, on the same day-of-month as your start date. Full cost only; no partial amounts.

Weekly / Bi-Weekly - Every 7 or 14 days from start. Every event landing in the month is counted at full cost.

Quarterly / Semi-Annual / Annual - Anniversary billing dates. Only events that fall within the displayed month count.

Yearly total - Sum of all billing events across the 12 months of the year. Subscriptions starting Jul–Dec will show a lower yearly total for their start year (fewer events before year-end).

Trial - Billing events during the trial window cost $0.

Cancelled - Events after the cancel date are excluded.

Per-cell currency - Each Cost cell can be in its own currency (USD/EUR/AED/HKD/…). All sums roll up to USD first, then convert to your chosen display currency.
