How to use the Expense Tracker

  • Add expense categories as rows via Table ▾ → + Row; add subcategories with +Sub on any row.
  • Enter spending amounts per cell and navigate months with the ◀ Prev / Next ▶ buttons.
  • Undo/Redo via Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y or the ↩↪ toolbar buttons (history persists across page visits).
  • Use the Share ▾ menu to share a snapshot, export to CSV/JSON, or paste imported data.
  • Set your gross income and monthly tax in the Monthly Budget panel above the table to track remaining budget.
  • Click 🎯 next to any row total to set a monthly spending limit - the total turns green / amber / red as spending approaches or exceeds your goal.
  • Forecast mode: future months let you project spend or copy/average last month's values.
